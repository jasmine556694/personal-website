About Me

I'm a passionate developer with a love for creating beautiful and functional web experiences.

My Journey

My programming journey began with a curiosity for how websites worked. This led me down the rabbit hole of HTML, CSS, and JavaScript, and I was immediately hooked. I spent countless hours building small projects, learning from online tutorials, and reading documentation to absorb as much as I could.

After mastering the fundamentals, I dove into modern frameworks like React and Vue.js, which opened up a new world of possibilities for creating dynamic and interactive applications. I'm driven by the challenge of solving complex problems and the satisfaction of seeing my code come to life.

Today, I specialize in full-stack development, comfortable with both crafting pixel-perfect user interfaces and architecting robust backend systems. I am constantly learning and exploring new technologies to stay at the forefront of the ever-evolving world of web development.
