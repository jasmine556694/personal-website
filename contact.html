Frequently Asked Questions

Here are some of the most common questions I get. If you don't find your answer here, feel free to send me a message.

What services do you offer?

I specialize in front-end development, focusing on creating responsive, high-performance websites using modern technologies like React, Vue, and Tailwind CSS. I also offer full-stack development with Node.js.

How can I start a project with you?

The best way to start is by sending me a message through the contact form with a brief description of your project. I'll get back to you within a couple of business days to schedule a consultation.

What is your typical project timeline?

Project timelines can vary greatly depending on the scope and complexity. A simple landing page might take 1-2 weeks, while a full-featured web application could take several months. We'll define a clear timeline after our initial consultation.

Do you work with international clients?

Absolutely! I work with clients from all over the world. We can easily coordinate through video calls and project management tools to ensure smooth communication regardless of time zones.
